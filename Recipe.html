Fried Rice Recipe

What you will need:

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tbs sesame oil
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 cup frozen veggies (thawed)
  • 3 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 2 stalks green onion (chopped)

Now to take the ingredients and put them together!

Directions

  1. Preheat a large skillet or wok to medium heat.
  2. Pour sesame oil in the bottom. Add the white onion and the thawed peas and carrots, fry until tender.
  3. Slide the onion, peas and carrots to one side of the pan, and pour the beaten eggs onto the other side. Using a spatula, scramble the eggs. Once cooked, mix the eggs with the vegetable mix.
  4. Add the rice to the veggie and egg mixture. Pour the soy sauce on top. Stir and fry the rice and veggie mixture until heated through and combined.
  5. Lastly, add chopped green onions and serve.

Enjoy!
